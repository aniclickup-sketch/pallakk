Our Story

Born from Kerala's Heart

✦

Pallakk — the name echoes with the spirit of Kerala's Palakkad region, where fertile paddy fields meet ancient spice routes. Under the visionary banner of the Appus Group, Pallakk was born to celebrate the unparalleled richness of Kerala's vegetarian culinary tradition.

Our recipes have been painstakingly curated from generations of Kerala households — from the grand sadhya feasts of Thrissur to the humble puttu and kadala of Malabar mornings. We believe that vegetarian food is not a limitation but an infinite canvas of flavour, colour, and culture.

Every ingredient is sourced from Kerala's own farms. Every spice is freshly ground. Every dish is cooked with the patience and love that only tradition can teach.
